## Cold Email in Germany: Practical Reality

UWG §7 technically requires consent for commercial email. However, the practical reality:

- **Enforcement is rare for B2B-to-B2B.** Risk is via competitor Abmahnung, not government action.
- **Virtually all German B2B companies do cold email outreach.** Tools like Lemlist, Apollo, Instantly are widely used.
- **Practical risk mitigation:**
  - Target carefully (don't blast; use trigger signals to justify outreach)
  - Include clear sender identity and easy opt-out
  - Keep a documented rationale per campaign (funding round, hiring signal, etc.)
  - Use professional, relevant messaging (not spam)
  - Remove anyone who opts out immediately
- **Bottom line:** Cold email is a viable and widely-used channel. The legal framework creates a small Abmahnung risk that is manageable with targeted, professional outreach.

## Cold Email Outreach Playbook

### Sequence Structure (3-5 touches, 10-14 days, multi-channel)

| Touch | Day | Channel | Content |
|-------|-----|---------|---------|
| 1 | 0 | Email | Pain-focused. Reference their trigger signal. |
| 2 | 3 | LinkedIn | Connection request with personalized note |
| 3 | 5 | Email | Case study or data point |
| 4 | 8 | Phone/LinkedIn | Brief, direct follow-up |
| 5 | 12 | Email | Breakup email with resource |

### Value Props by Persona

- **CFO/Finance:** OpEx vs CapEx, no fit-out costs, lease flexibility
- **HR/People Ops:** Employee experience, locations near talent, hybrid enablement
- **Office Manager:** Turnkey solution, scale up/down, no facility management
- **CEO/Founder:** Speed to market, focus on core business, capital preservation

### Tools

| Tool | Strength | Pricing |
|------|---------|---------|
| Lemlist | AI personalization, dynamic images, multi-channel | From €39/mo |
| Instantly.ai | Email warmup + sending at scale | From $30/mo |
| Apollo.io | All-in-one (data + sequences) — weaker DACH data | From $49/mo |
| LaGrowthMachine | Multi-channel (LinkedIn + Email), EU-first | From €60/mo |

### Risk Mitigation
- Target carefully using trigger signals (don't blast)
- Clear sender identity + easy opt-out in every email
- Document rationale per campaign
- Remove opt-outs immediately
- Professional, relevant messaging (not spam)
- Keep volumes reasonable (50-100/week per domain, not thousands)

"Demand aggregators will face challenging times as direct business increases" — operators improving at direct sales. Brokerage value must shift from "we list you" to "we bring qualified enterprise leads you can't reach on your own." Advisory services are the long-term differentiator.

## Key Strategic Insights

1. **Your biggest untapped asset is existing traffic.** Dealfront + calculators + lead magnets can turn stalled SEO into a lead machine without more visitors.

2. **Cold email works in practice.** Everyone does it. Use trigger signals to target carefully and keep volumes professional.

3. **Broker-to-broker is the highest-leverage partnership.** 95% of large flex office deals come through CRE brokers. Build referral networks with Colliers/JLL/CBRE.

4. **Signals beat spray-and-pray.** Funding rounds + hiring velocity + website visits + Handelsregister = precise targeting. Call/email 50 companies with reasons, not 5,000 with hope.

5. **DACH-native tools win.** Dealfront and Cognism have 2-5x better German data than ZoomInfo, Apollo, or other US-centric platforms.

6. **Aggregator risk is real.** Long-term, evolve toward advisory/managed office services (Instant Group model) for larger deal sizes and stickier relationships.
