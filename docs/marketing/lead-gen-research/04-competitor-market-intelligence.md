# Competitive Intelligence & Market Research — DACH Flex Office Brokerage

## Research Report — February 2026

---

## 1. COMPETITOR LANDSCAPE

### Direct Competitors (DACH)
- **shareDnC** (Cologne) — peer-to-peer subletting, 2,000+ companies served
- **MatchOffice** (Copenhagen, active in Germany) — 47,000+ spaces globally
- **Spacebase** (Berlin) — meeting rooms and event spaces, 4,000+ venues
- **Workaround.io** — German coworking cost comparison, content-driven SEO

### International Players in DACH
- **Workthere** (Savills subsidiary) — 632+ flex spaces in Germany, free to occupiers
- **The Instant Group / Coworker.com** (IWG) — 25,000+ spaces, ~$86.4M revenue
- **Office Freedom** — global brokerage, active in Germany

### CRE Giants with Flex Divisions
- **Colliers SmartFlex** — 90 global markets
- **JLL** — dedicated flex advisory teams in Germany
- **CBRE** — 36% of clients report >10% portfolio in flex

### Operators as Pseudo-Competitors
- **IWG/Regus/Spaces** — ~130 locations Germany, 70+ new planned
- **WeWork** — strong brand in Berlin, Munich, Frankfurt, Hamburg
- **Design Offices** — German-specific, ~50 locations (being acquired by IWG)
- **Mindspace** — growing German footprint

---

## 2. INTERNATIONAL BENCHMARKS

### UK Market (Most Mature)
- **Hubble HQ** (acquired by Yardi Jan 2025) — 15% enquiry-to-deal conversion (vs 5-6% industry avg)
- **Instant Group** — revenue ~$86.4M, merged with IWG + Coworker.com

**Key insight:** UK market has consolidated heavily. This wave may come to DACH.

### US Market
- **LiquidSpace** — sends "millions of emails," heavy SEO investment
- **SquareFoot/Upsuite** — 95 of 100 largest WeWork transactions came through CRE brokers

**Key insight:** Broker referral partnerships are proven highest-value channel for large deals.

---

## 3. MARKET SIZE & GROWTH

- Germany coworking: USD 492.5M (2023) → USD 1,467M by 2030 (CAGR 16.8%)
- Europe flex office: USD 13.28B (2025) → USD 22.65B by 2030
- Berlin: 330,000 sqm flex | Munich: 242,000 sqm | Frankfurt: 150,000 sqm
- 1,800+ coworking spaces, 70% under 500 sqm

### Pricing (Deal Value Context)
- Berlin: Hot desk €150-300/mo, Private office €400-1,000/desk/mo
- Munich: Hot desk from €150/mo, Private €400-1,200/desk/mo
- Frankfurt: €400-600/desk/mo typical

---

## 4. REVENUE MODELS

| Model | Typical Rate | Used By |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Provider Commission | 10-20% first year | Hubble, Workthere |
| Transaction Fee | 10% monthly | LiquidSpace |
| Broker Referral | 10% months 1-12 | WeWork, IWG |
| Lead Fee | €20-100/lead | Some platforms |
| Managed Advisory | Retainer + success | Instant Group |

---

## 5. DEAL ECONOMICS

| Scenario | Desks | Monthly Spend | Annual | Commission (15%) |
|----------|-------|---------------|--------|-------------------|
| Small team | 5 | €2,000 | €24,000 | €3,600 |
| Mid team | 15 | €6,000 | €72,000 | €10,800 |
| Large team | 50 | €20,000 | €240,000 | €36,000 |

**To reach €1.5M revenue:** ~100-300 deals/year at €5-15K commission (2-6 deals/week)

---

## 6. CRITICAL RISK — AGGREGATOR HEADWINDS

2026 marks "accelerated consolidation and growing professionalization" in Germany. Demand aggregators face challenging times as operators improve direct sales. **Value proposition must shift from "we list you" to "we bring qualified enterprise leads you can't reach on your own."**
