# Outbound Sales & Partnership Lead Generation — DACH

## Research Report — February 2026

---

## PART 1: OUTBOUND CHANNELS

### 1. Cold Email Outreach

**Potential:** Medium-High volume, Medium-High quality | **Time to results:** 4-8 weeks

**Legal note:** UWG §7 technically restricts cold email, but in practice B2B cold email is widespread in Germany. Risk mitigation: target carefully using trigger signals, include clear opt-out, document rationale per campaign, keep volumes reasonable.

**Data sources for list building:**

| Source | Cost | DACH Coverage |
|--------|------|---------------|
| Dealfront | Custom, from EUR 99/mo | Best-in-class |
| Cognism | EUR 1,500-25,000/yr | Strong, 200M+ European contacts |
| Apollo.io | From USD 49/mo | Weaker DACH |
| LinkedIn Sales Navigator | EUR 80-130/mo | Excellent |
| GrowthList | Subscription | 478+ funded Berlin startups |

**Recommended DACH stack:** Dealfront + Cognism + LinkedIn Sales Navigator

**Sequence structure:** 3-5 touches over 10-14 days, multi-channel (email + LinkedIn + phone)

**Value props by persona:**
- CFO: OpEx vs CapEx, lease flexibility
- HR: Employee experience, hybrid enablement
- Office Manager: Turnkey, scale up/down
- CEO/Founder: Speed, capital preservation

**Tools:** Lemlist (from €39/mo), Instantly.ai (from $30/mo), LaGrowthMachine (from €60/mo)

**Cost:** EUR 300-500/mo (minimum viable) to EUR 5,000-8,000/mo (full build with SDR)

### 2. LinkedIn Outbound

**Potential:** High quality, Medium volume | **Time to results:** 2-6 weeks

**Sales Navigator prospecting workflow:**
1. Saved Searches for Office Managers / HR / COOs at 20-200 employee companies with headcount growth
2. Spotlight filters for recent job changers, LinkedIn posters
3. Personalized connection requests (300 char limit)
4. Follow up with InMail or message

**Automation tools:** Expandi (€99/mo), LaGrowthMachine (€60/mo) — cloud-based, human-mimicking

**Cost:** EUR 200-300/mo + time

---

### 3. Account-Based Marketing (ABM)

**Potential:** Low volume, Very High quality | **Time to results:** 3-6 months

Best for 50-200 employee companies taking 10+ desks. Multi-channel campaigns over 5 weeks: LinkedIn + email + retargeting ads + direct mail + phone.

**Cost:** EUR 1,000-2,500/mo (DIY) to EUR 3,000-10,000/mo (with agency)

---

### 4. Cold Calling

**Potential:** Medium volume, High quality | **Time to results:** 1-4 weeks

B2B cold calling is legally permissible in Germany with "presumed consent." Most effective as part of multi-channel sequence. Call 09:00-11:30 and 14:00-16:00. Lead with the trigger.

## PART 3: TRIGGER-BASED OUTREACH

### 12. Funding Announcements — #1 TRIGGER
Reach out within 1-2 weeks. Sources: Crunchbase Pro ($49/mo), Dealroom, deutsche-startups.de, Google Alerts.

### 13. Job Posting Signals
Companies posting 10+ roles or "Office Manager" hires. Monitor via LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Coresignal, LoneScale.

### 14. Company Relocations
Google Alerts, LinkedIn, local business press, IHK announcements.

### 15. Lease Expiry Data
Not publicly available in Germany. Build proprietary database from prospect conversations over time.
